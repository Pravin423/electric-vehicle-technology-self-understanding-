Programme Overview

With the global EV market growing at 25%+ CAGR and India aiming for 30% EV adoption by 2030, the demand for skilled EV professionals is rising rapidly.

The 6-month Advanced Programme in Electric Vehicle Technology equips you with essential expertise in battery systems, electric drivetrains, power electronics, and charging infrastructure. From understanding storage devices at the cell-to-pack level to exploring smart grids and system integration, the programme offers a complete foundation in e-mobility. Designed for engineers and working professionals, it helps you build future-ready skills and accelerate your career in the fast-evolving EV industry.
