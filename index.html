Programme Overview

With the global EV market growing at 25%+ CAGR and India aiming for 30% EV adoption by 2030, the demand for skilled EV professionals is rising rapidly.

The 6-month Advanced Programme in Electric Vehicle Technology equips you with essential expertise in battery systems, electric drivetrains, power electronics, and charging infrastructure. From understanding storage devices at the cell-to-pack level to exploring smart grids and system integration, the programme offers a complete foundation in e-mobility. Designed for engineers and working professionals, it helps you build future-ready skills and accelerate your career in the fast-evolving EV industry.

Eligibility Criteria

  • For Indian Participants – Graduates or Diploma Holders from a recognised University / Institutes (UGC / AICTE / DEC / AIU / State Government) in any discipline.
  • For International Participants – Graduation or equivalent degree from any recognised University or Institution in their respective country.
  • Attendance: Minimum 50% attendance is mandatory in live sessions.

Who Should Attend

  • Fresh Graduates who aspire to work in the sunrise sector of Electric Vehicle (EV) technology.
  • Entry-Level R&D Professionals seeking understanding of energy storage devices and power electronics.
  • Mid & Senior-Level Professionals looking to refresh core principles and future trends.
  • Quality Control Professionals aiming to understand the relationship between specifications and EV components.
  • Production, Sales & Support Teams who need clarity on rating, sizing, and powertrain drives.
  • Management Roles seeking familiarity with EV jargon and technology limitations.
